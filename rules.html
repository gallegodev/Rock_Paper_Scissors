Anubis Game


Once every 100 years, the god Anubis selects mortals from around the world to compete in a deadly Rock, Paper, Scissors challenge.


Those who lose the challenge will give their souls to the God Anubis, however the winners will have any wish granted.


However to win the game you have to obey the rules:


The game is played with three possible hand signals that represent a rock, paper, and scissors.


  • Rock is a closed fist and wins against scissors;
  • paper is a flat hand with fingers and thumb extended and the palm facing downward and wins against rock;
  • scissors is a fist with the index and middle fingers fully extended and wins against paper
  • If both players throw the same hand signal, it is considered a tie, and play resumes until there is a clear winner.


Praise be the strongest challenger!
